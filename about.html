About

The 1% Match is a free World Cup football trivia game. Twelve questions, each harder than the last, with 100 virtual contestants being eliminated along the way. Your goal is simple: be the 1% that makes it to the end.

There's no sign-up. No app to download. It runs entirely in your browser at onepercentmatch.com. Open it on your phone, tablet, or laptop and start playing immediately.

The game is built for football fans who want a quick daily challenge or a longer session with friends. Play the Daily Challenge and compare your results, go back and try Previous Days you missed, grind Unlimited mode for practice, or set up a Multiplayer room and see who actually knows their stuff.

Everything is stored locally in your browser. Your stats, your streaks, your game history -- it all stays on your device. No accounts, no servers tracking you, no data leaving your machine.

If you have questions, feedback, or just want to say hello, drop a line at onepercentmatch@outlook.com.

The 1% Match is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to FIFA, any national football association, any football club, or any other official organisation. All World Cup facts and trivia are used for entertainment purposes only.
