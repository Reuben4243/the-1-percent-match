How to Play

The 1% Match is a World Cup football trivia game built around one idea: progressive elimination. You start alongside 100 virtual contestants and try to be the last one standing.

The Concept

100 virtual contestants enter the game. There are 12 questions, each harder than the last. After every question, a chunk of contestants are eliminated based on the difficulty tier. By the final question, only 1% remain. Your goal is to answer all 12 correctly and be among them.

Question Tiers

Each question has a pass rate that determines how many contestants survive it. The further you go, the fewer make it through.

If you get a question wrong, you're out -- just like the virtual contestants who didn't make the cut.

The Timer

You have 15 seconds to answer each question. The timer bar at the top of the screen shows how much time you have left. It turns yellow when you're running low, and red in the final seconds. If the timer runs out, it counts as a wrong answer.

The Pass

From Question 5 onwards, a Pass button appears. Using it lets you skip a question you're unsure about -- but it costs you your £1,000 stake. You keep your place in the game, but your potential prize drops. You only get one Pass per game, so use it wisely.

The Final Decision

If you make it to Question 12, you face a choice before answering:

  • Play for the pot -- answer Q12 and, if you get it right, win the full prize pool.
  • Take £10,000 -- walk away with a guaranteed £10,000 without risking Q12.

If multiple finalists answer Q12 correctly, the pot is split equally between them.

Game Modes

  • Daily Challenge -- a new set of 12 questions every day. Same questions for everyone, so you can compare with friends.
  • Previous Days -- missed a day? Go back and play any past daily challenge.
  • Unlimited -- play as many games as you like with randomised questions. Great for practice.
  • Multiplayer -- create a room and play the same questions at the same time with friends. See who survives the longest.

Streaks & Stats

The game tracks your stats locally in the browser -- no account needed. You can see your current streak, best streak, total games played, and how often you make it to different stages. Come back every day to keep your streak alive.
