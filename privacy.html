Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Short Version

The 1% Match does not collect your personal data. There are no accounts, no servers storing your information, and no tracking. Everything the game saves is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

No Accounts, No Server

The game runs entirely in your browser. There is no backend server, no database, and no user accounts. Your stats, streaks, and game history are saved in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. No names, no email addresses, no IP addresses, no device fingerprints. We do not know who you are, and we have no way to identify you.

No Cookies

The 1% Match does not set any cookies. The game uses localStorage for saving your progress and preferences, which is not the same as cookies and is not shared with any third party.

No Analytics

There are currently no analytics tools, tracking scripts, or usage monitoring of any kind on this site. This may change in the future, and this policy will be updated if it does.

No Ads

There are no advertisements on The 1% Match. No ad networks, no ad trackers, no sponsored content.

Third-Party Services

The only external service used is Google Fonts, which loads the Inter typeface. When the page loads, your browser makes a request to Google's font servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). Google's own privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services are used.

Clearing Your Data

Since all game data is stored in your browser's localStorage, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser data. In most browsers, go to Settings, then Privacy or Storage, and clear site data for onepercentmatch.com. This will reset your stats, streaks, and game history.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out at hello@onepercentmatch.com.
